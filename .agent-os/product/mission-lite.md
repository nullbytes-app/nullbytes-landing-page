# Product Mission (Lite)

Nullbytes is a cyberpunk-themed landing page that helps potential clients and collaborators discover AI development expertise by providing an immersive showcase of AI projects, team portfolios, and direct contact channels with cutting-edge visual design.

Nullbytes serves tech startup founders, AI developers, and project idea holders who need to connect with specialized AI development talent. Unlike generic developer portfolios, Nullbytes provides a fully immersive cyberpunk experience with sophisticated animations that immediately communicates technical sophistication and creates memorable brand recognition for the AI collective.
